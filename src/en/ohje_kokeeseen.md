### Retakes and separate exams

Retakes are arranged separately as agreed (possibly on general exam days, provided that no material exam is scheduled).

### Course exam

The course exam will be held on December 18. The exam has a response time of 3 hours from the moment you start the exam. Please note that regardless of when you start the exam, it will end at 11:59 p.m.

The internet is freely available during the exam.

It is worth noting that the exam and its \"correct answers\" are based on the course material. This means that even if you find the answer to a question by Googling a random page, if it uses different terminology than the course or otherwise differs radically from what is presented in the course, the answer may not earn you many points.

### Exam scope

The exam covers sections 1-5 of the course material and the calculators, except for

- Git
- poetry
- GitHub Actions
- unittest
- Robot/Selenium

Guest lectures are not included in the exam area.

The exam is similar to previous years\' exams for the University of Helsinki course, e.g.

- [autumn 2022](koe2022.md)
- [fall](koe2021.md)
