In the course, we cover the basics of software production project management, work stages and tools, emphasizing agile software production methods. 

The course teaching arrangements and grading criteria are described in [part 0](osa0.md), read them carefully at the beginning of the course!

### Course feedback

In addition to the feedback collected at the end of the course, you can give anonymous feedback to the course staff at any time at <https://norppa.jyu.fi/targets/7131/feedback>.




## Staff

Petri Ihantola and Simo Lehtinen serve as the course's responsible teachers, and Niko Iljin as the course assistant. You can email the staff at ohtu1@tim.jyu.fi (e.g. course equivalencies or personal arrangements).

## Guidance

The course Discord channel is located on the same server as several other courses: <https://discord.gg/Fd5Bq36V6U>

In addition, you can get help with the assignments at [Koodaamo][https://tim.pm/Koodaamo], Agoran Latin computer classroom (Ag B112.2) on Tuesdays, Thursdays, and Fridays from 12:00 PM to 2:00 PM. The course's own assistant is present on Fridays, but you can participate in Koodaamo at other times as well. 

In addition, students of the Software Production course can participate in joint exercises with the [Software Testing][https://opencs.it.jyu.fi/software-testing/] course on Mondays and Thursdays from 9:15 AM to 10:45 AM in room C331.3.

Participation in the groups is voluntary, and you can come to do assignments and ask the teachers for help. No email advice is provided for completing the assignments.
