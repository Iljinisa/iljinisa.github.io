

The course covers the basics of software production project management, work phases, and tools, with an emphasis on agile software production methods.

The course teaching arrangements and assessment criteria are described [in section 0](osa0.md). Please read them carefully at the beginning of the course!

Guest lectures on Wednesday, December 10, from 10:15 a.m. to 12:00 p.m. (participation earns course credits related to guest lectures)
