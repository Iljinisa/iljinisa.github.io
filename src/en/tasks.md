The course includes weekly calculation exercises. There are *two types of* assignments

- *weekly multiple-choice exercises* that review the theory covered in the lectures and course materials, with a deadline of Sunday at 23:59:00
- The second part of the assignments deals with more technical aspects of software production, such as version management, testing, and software configuration. The deadline for these is *Monday at 11:59 p.m.*

The impact of the assignments on the course grade is explained [in section 0](osa0.md#kurssin-arvostelu).

The course\'s Discord channel is located on the same server as a few other courses: <https://discord.gg/Fd5Bq36V6U>

In addition, help with the assignments is available at \[Koodaamo\]\[https://tim.pm/Koodaamo\], Agora Latin computer classroom (Ag B112.2) on Tuesdays, Thursdays, and Fridays from 12:00 p.m. to 2:00 p.m. The course assistant is available on Fridays, but you can also visit Koodaamo at other times.

In addition, students of the Software Production course can participate in joint exercises with the \[Software Development\]\[https://opencs.it.jyu.fi/software-testing/\] course on Mondays and Thursdays from 9:15 to 10:45 in classroom C331.3.

Participation in the groups is voluntary, and you can come to do assignments and ask teachers for help. No email support is provided for assignments.

### Course feedback

In addition to the feedback collected at the end of the course, you can give anonymous feedback to the course staff at any time at <https://norppa.jyu.fi/targets/7131/feedback>.
